What You'll Learn

Expertify

Expert status is the only status that matters. You may not feel like an expert at first, so you'll discover what to do about it.

Onboardify

Bringing a client on board starts in the initial contact, not after the sale. The overriding goal will be to establish that "I am the expert, I will run this show and solve your problems for you."

Respectify

As a future consultant, you must aim to get the same respect for your time, education, training, and skills from your client as he has for his surgeon.

Teammify

A great team will make you money. An OK team will cause you stress. A cheap team will hold you back and cost you money. You'll learn how to build a great team.

Futurify

You can make huge sweeping changes to your business with little risk to next month's cash flow. How? Apply your changes to new future deals only.

Nichify

By saying "no" to that WordPress theme creation and "no" to that MailChimp email integration, you are telling the world that you only say "yes" to projects that fit your vision.
